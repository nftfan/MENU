X (formerly Twitter) remains one of the world’s most widely used social platforms, with hundreds of millions of users sharing millions of posts daily. Here’s a **data-driven snapshot** of its total user base, verified accounts, and monetization dynamics as of 2025 and early 2026.

Total Users on X

X has around **600–650 million monthly active users** and **240–300 million daily active users** worldwide. This user base forms the foundation for verification and monetization.

Verified Accounts

Verified accounts — indicated by the blue checkmark — number roughly **1 million**, which is approximately **0.15–0.17%** of total users. Verification is often tied to premium subscriptions and public recognition.

Who Gets Verified?

Verification covers public figures, creators, organizations, and paying subscribers who meet X’s criteria. Premium accounts can opt-in for verification, while some high-profile users receive it automatically.

Monetization & Earnings

Monetization requires verified status and sufficient engagement. Only a small subset of verified users — well under 1% — actually earn meaningful income from subscriptions, creator programs, or tips.
