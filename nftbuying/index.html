How to buy $NFTFAN

Sale live
1

Get POL in your wallet

You need POL (the native token of Polygon) to buy. Buy POL on any exchange — Binance, Coinbase, Kraken — and withdraw it to your wallet on the Polygon network.

2

Send POL to the sale contract

From your own wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rabby…), send the amount of POL you want to spend to the sale contract address below. Never send from an exchange account — tokens would go to the exchange's wallet, not yours.

3

Receive $NFTFAN automatically

The sale contract sends $NFTFAN back to the same wallet in the same transaction. No claiming, no extra steps.

4

Verify your new balance

Wait ~30 seconds for the transaction to confirm, then paste your address above (or hit Refresh) to see your updated balance.
