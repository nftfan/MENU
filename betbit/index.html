How to Play
1Connect your wallet — the app auto-switches you to the Polygon network.
2Pay 1 POL to start a session. This goes into the on-chain prize pool.
3Tap UP or DOWN. Your entry price locks at that instant and a 20-second timer starts.
4When the timer ends, if BTC moved your way it's a WIN; otherwise (or a flat tie) it's a LOSS.
55 wins in a row → you win the 5 POL prize, sent by the game owner from the contract.
62 losses in a row → game over. Pay 1 POL again to start a fresh session.
⚑Rounds within a paid session are free — you only pay 1 POL to begin each new session.
🎮Try for free lets you play one practice round with no payment. It won't count toward the prize — even a win here is not added to the winners list.
