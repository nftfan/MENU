How to Buy NFTFAN

1Send $POL
Send between 0.01 and 100 $POL to the following smart contract address:
0xaf2d132C8773bca3821C24EcF64e844E202A12e8
Make sure to send from a wallet that you control, not from an exchange.
2Wait for Transaction
  • After sending, wait for your transaction to be confirmed on the Polygon network (usually less than a minute).
  • Your $NFTFAN tokens will be automatically sent to the same wallet you used for sending $POL.
3Add $NFTFAN to Your Wallet
Add the $NFTFAN token contract address to your wallet:
0x2017Fcaea540d2925430586DC92818035Bfc2F50
This will allow you to see your $NFTFAN balance in your wallet.
