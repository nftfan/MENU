Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my NFTFAN tokens?

Tokens are usually sent automatically within seconds after your POL transaction is confirmed. In rare cases, during high network congestion, it might take a few minutes.

Is there a minimum or maximum purchase amount?

Yes, you can send between 0.01 and 100 POL to the airdrop contract. The amount of NFTFAN tokens you receive will be proportional to your POL contribution.

What can I do with NFTFAN tokens?

NFTFAN tokens are the foundation of our decentralized advertising ecosystem. They can be used for purchasing ad space, supporting creators, participating in governance, and as an investment in the growing NFT advertising space.

Where can I trade NFTFAN tokens?

Currently, NFTFAN is in its early distribution phase. Trading options and exchange listings will be announced on our official channels as they become available.

How does the NFT Fans ecosystem work?

The NFT Fans ecosystem connects creators, advertisers, and users in a decentralized network where content creators earn fair compensation for their work. Advertisers gain access to targeted audiences, while users maintain control over their data and can earn rewards for participation.
