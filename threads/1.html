NFT FANS TOKEN: Revolutionizing the World of NFT Ads
NFT FANS TOKEN ($NFTF) is a groundbreaking utility token on the APECHAIN, designed to transform the NFT advertising ecosystem. Here's a thread explaining its vision, features, and how it empowers both big companies and individual distributors.
1/ What is NFT FANS TOKEN?
NFT FANS TOKEN ($NFTF) is a cryptocurrency built on the APECHAIN blockchain. It aims to facilitate a decentralized advertising platform where NFT ads are distributed through volunteers known as "distributors." These distributors earn rewards by sending NFT ads to others.
2/ How does NFT FANS TOKEN work?
The platform enables big companies to mint and distribute NFT ads to a large number of users. Distributors (volunteers) will send these NFT ads to others and earn NFTF tokens as rewards, which they can use or trade.
