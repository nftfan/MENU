Huawei Unveils First Triple-Foldable Phone, the Mate XT Ultimate Design

NFTFANS TOKEN | PUBLISHED ON 14 September 2024 | Author: NFTFANS

Huawei has launched the world’s first triple-foldable phone, the Mate XT Ultimate Design, setting a new standard in foldable technology. Priced at $2,800, this cutting-edge device features a foldable screen that can transform into a 10.2-inch tablet, offering both dual and triple-screen modes.

The Mate XT's innovative design is powered by flexible materials and a unique hinge system, allowing it to fold in both directions. With four cameras, including a periscope telephoto lens, and a 5,600-mAh battery, the Mate XT caters to high-end users seeking the latest tech.

Despite its hefty price, Huawei has already received over 4 million preorders, solidifying its position as a leader in the global foldable market, surpassing Samsung. As foldables continue to rise in popularity, Huawei’s latest innovation is a glimpse into the future of mobile technology.
