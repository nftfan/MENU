iPhone 17 Models to Feature Major Display Upgrades

NFTFANS TOKEN | PUBLISHED ON 20 September 2024 | Author: NFTFANS

As anticipation builds for next year's iPhone 17 lineup, rumors suggest that 2025 could be a significant year for display technology across all models. According to analyst Ross Young, both the standard iPhone 17 and the new iPhone 17 Air will receive several Pro-level features, including the highly sought-after always-on display and ProMotion, which offers a smoother 120Hz refresh rate.

While the ultra-slim iPhone 17 Air is expected to take center stage with its sleek design and premium pricing, the inclusion of these features in the standard iPhone 17 is a welcome surprise. This move brings Apple’s high-end display technologies to a wider audience, making it more tempting to skip the pricier Pro models.

Speaking of the Pro models, the iPhone 17 Pro and Pro Max are rumored to introduce under-display Face ID, a long-awaited feature. This advancement could eliminate the Dynamic Island cutout, replacing it with a smaller, more discreet hole-punch design.

With these upgrades on the horizon, the iPhone 17 is shaping up to be a compelling release, offering more advanced display features across the board.
