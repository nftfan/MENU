Apple Watch SE With Colorful Plastic Design Still in Development

NFTFANS TOKEN | PUBLISHED ON 16 September 2024 | Author: NFTFANS

Apple is reportedly developing a new version of the Apple Watch SE that will feature a colorful plastic design, reminiscent of the iPhone 5c from 2013. According to Bloomberg's Mark Gurman, this upcoming model remains in the works despite facing some challenges related to cost and quality due to the shift from aluminum to plastic.

The new Apple Watch SE aims to be more affordable, potentially appealing to parent's looking for a budget-friendly option for their children. Currently, the 2022 Apple Watch SE starts at $249 in the U.S., making it a mid-range choice among wearable devices. The inclusion of a plastic casing could help reduce production costs and offer a more vibrant and varied color palette.

In addition to the new design, the next Apple Watch SE is expected to come with a new chip, providing enhanced performance over its predecessor. This upgrade could bring faster processing speeds and improved functionality, making the watch more competitive in the market.

As Apple continues to refine this project, it's likely that further details will emerge, potentially ahead of a planned release next year.
